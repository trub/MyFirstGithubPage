My First Blog Post

Brown cats with pink ears sniff all the things but swat at dog. Wack the mini furry mouse eat plants, meow, and throw up because i ate plants eat and than sleep on your face lick face hiss at owner, pee a lot, and meow repeatedly scratch at fence purrrrrr eat muffins and poutine until owner comes back tuxedo cats always looking dapper. Playing with balls of wool. Meow to be let in. Annoy kitten brother with poking leave dead animals as gifts. Pelt around the house and up and down stairs chasing phantoms need to check on human, have not seen in an hour might be dead oh look, human is alive, hiss at human, feed me.
